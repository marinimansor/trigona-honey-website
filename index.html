The Amazing Health Benefits

Trigona (Kelulut) honey is widely recognized by scientists and traditional healers as a superfood due to its unique medicinal properties.

4x - 10x Antioxidants

Contains exceptionally high levels of phenolic acids and flavonoids compared to standard honeybee honey. It aggressively combats free radicals and slows cellular aging.

Propolis-Infused Defense

Because Trigona bees store their honey in pots made of propolis (bee glue) rather than wax, the honey absorbs powerful antifungal, antibacterial, and antiviral compounds directly.

Low Glycemic Index (GI)

Fructose and complex sugars dominate, causing a slower, more stable release of glucose into the bloodstream. It provides clean, sustained energy without the harsh insulin spikes.

Immune & Throat Relief

A natural sore throat remedy and cough suppressant. Its active bioflavonoids work to inhibit common respiratory pathogens and strengthen your daily immune response.

Gut Health & Enzymes

Loaded with active enzymes and natural prebiotics, Kelulut honey helps improve digestion, eases acid reflux, and fosters a healthy balance of beneficial gut bacteria.

Unique Sweet & Sour Taste

Due to the natural fermentation process occurring in the propolis pots, Trigona honey possesses an elegant, tangy, citrus-like flavor that is lighter and more refreshing than standard honey.

From Our Farm To Your Table

How we ensure you receive 100% authentic, premium-grade Trigona honey.

1

Log Hive Care

Our stingless bee colonies thrive in natural wood log hives placed in an organic orchard, far away from urban pollution.

2

Propolis Maturation

Bees build resinous propolis pots to store honey. Over weeks, the honey ferments slightly and absorbs therapeutic propolis compounds.

3

Gentle Extraction

We harvest small batches using a clean, food-grade suction pump, taking care never to damage the bees or the nest structure.

4

Raw Packaging

No boiling, no added sugar, and no preservatives. The honey is coarsely filtered to remove debris and immediately bottled raw.
